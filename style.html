About this years carnival

This year's Kogi International Carnival 2024 is set to be an extraordinary celebration across the state, with festivities happening in key locations such as Idah, Okene, and Kabba. From 1st to 7th December, Kogi will come alive with colorful parades, vibrant costumes, and exhilarating performances from both local and international artists. Expect exciting activities such as cultural dances, masquerade shows, boat regattas on the Niger River in Idah, traditional wrestling in Okene, and art exhibitions in Kabba, all showcasing the rich heritage of Kogi State. Whether you're drawn to the electrifying street parades, the vibrant music, or the authentic food markets, the Kogi International Carnival will offer something for everyone. Get ready for a week of unforgettable experiences that celebrate the spirit and diversity of our culture!
